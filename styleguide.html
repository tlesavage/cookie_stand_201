Brand Guidance for Marketing Collateral

    Apparel

  • People love free stuff. Merchandise, aka "swag," can be an effective reminder of our brand. However, don't expect it to carry your entire message and compel your audience into action. Instead, it should be a part of the whole experience we provide. When designing identification elements for merchandise, keep it simple, and remember that our brand is the one that will make someone hold onto a pen or T-shirt.
